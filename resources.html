ProSoundWeb (External)

Selected technical articles by Jerrold Stevens
  • The Best Place to Put Subwoofers Is…
    Examines subwoofer placement strategies beyond conventional rules of thumb, focusing on low-frequency behavior and system integration.
  • The Relationship Between Amplifier Damping Factor, Impedance & Cable
    Explores how amplifier damping factor interacts with loudspeaker impedance and cable characteristics, and why these relationships matter in practice.
  • Conventional Wisdom: “I Can EQ a System Better by Ear”
    Challenges the notion that equalization by ear alone is sufficient, emphasizing measurement-based approaches and repeatable results.
  • Defining Amplifier Damping (And What It Really Means Within a System)
    A technical discussion of amplifier damping factor and its real-world implications for loudspeaker control and low-frequency performance.
