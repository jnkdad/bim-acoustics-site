Information We Collect

Our website and software do not collect personal information for advertising purposes.

If you enter an email address within an application to validate a license, that email address is used solely for licensing validation and customer support.

License Validation

License validation may transmit:

  • Application version and product identifier

Logs and Diagnostics

If you voluntarily submit logs or error reports, those logs may contain technical information such as Revit version, add-in version, and error messages.

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed through third-party providers (e.g., Stripe). BIM Acoustics does not store full payment card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information is shared only as necessary to provide licensing, payment processing, or support services.

Data Retention

Licensing and support records are retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal or accounting obligations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information associated with licensing and support.
