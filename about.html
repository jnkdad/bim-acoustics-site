About BIM Acoustics

Jerrold Stevens

Founder | AV & Acoustics Systems Design
Jerrold Stevens is the founder of BIM Acoustics and has spent decades working at the intersection of audio-video system design, acoustics, and professional education.
His background spans consulting practice, system design, and teaching, with a long-standing focus on sound reinforcement and distributed audio systems. Jerrold has taught and developed courses for industry organizations including InfoComm / AVIXA and has presented internationally across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.
In recent years, his work has centered on applying BIM methodologies to AV design—specifically using Revit to improve consistency, coordination, and efficiency in multi-room projects. BIM Acoustics tools are built directly from this experience, reflecting workflows used in practice rather than abstract theory.

David Marsh, FASA

President, Principal | Marsh/PMK International, LLC
David Marsh is President and Principal of Marsh/PMK International, LLC and a recognized leader in architectural acoustics and audio-video system consulting. His professional experience spans more than three decades across architectural acoustics, environmental noise, and AV systems design.
Mr. Marsh is a Fellow of the Acoustical Society of America (FASA) and a past president of the National Council of Acoustical Consultants. He has written extensively for industry publications, presented papers, chaired sessions at professional society meetings, and serves as a regular seminar instructor at international conferences in the U.S. and Asia.
His project experience includes hotels and mega-resorts, casinos, convention centers, retail/dining/entertainment venues, professional and collegiate sports facilities, theme parks and attractions, museums, performing arts centers, houses of worship, universities, K–12 schools, healthcare facilities, government buildings, high-rise office towers, and large-scale corporate and residential developments.
David collaborates with BIM Acoustics on acoustics-related tools, analysis workflows, and research-driven development.
